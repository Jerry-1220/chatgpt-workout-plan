✨ No‑BS Lean-Muscle Guide

Goal: add a modest, clean layer of muscle (shoulders, chest, arms, traps) while staying lean. Three focused sessions/week, mornings + weekend. Keep the Full‑Body plan as your busy‑week backup.

Core rules: Show up 3×/week 💯 · Hit ~120g protein 🥚🍗 · Add small progress weekly 📈 · Sleep 😴

💪 Main Plan — Push / Pull / Legs (8–12 weeks)

Rep zones: Compounds 8–10; accessories 10–15. Top end clean ➜ increase next time (smallest jump).

Deload (optional)Every 8–10 weeks, reduce weights ~15% for a weekPrevents burnout; joints happy

🥗 Nutrition — HK‑Friendly (Target ~120g protein)

Daily Targets

  • Protein: ~120 g/day
  • Calories: ~maintenance to +200–300 kcal
  • Water: 2–3 L/day

Easy Wins

  • Lunch: Cafe de Coral / Fairwood chicken breast set (~47–50 g)
  • Breakfast: 3 eggs + toast + fruit (~24 g)
  • Snack: whey scoop (20–25 g) or Greek yogurt (~10 g)
  • Dinner: rice + lean meat/fish/tofu (25–35 g)

🤝 Low‑Drama Gym Etiquette

  • Wear stuff you can move in; bring a towel; wipe benches.
  • Don’t hog a station scrolling; offer to “work in.”
  • Re‑rack plates & DBs properly.
  • Give people space; no unsolicited coaching.
  • Keep the noise reasonable; save theatrics for PR day.
  • Headphones on, head down = universal “I’m training.”
  • Be friendly but focused — you’re here to lift, not linger.

❓ Mini FAQ

How fast will I see changes?

Strength jumps in weeks; visible shape changes 6–10 weeks. Photos monthly, same lighting.

Do I need cardio?

You bike daily already. Optional light sessions help recovery; don’t overdo.

What if a machine’s different?

Match the motion pattern and cues; note new seat/pin numbers in your log.
